Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to the number of players I can get on a server?

You can have an unlimited number of players on your server and we don't ask for an extra charge for it. Every one of our plans comes with no player amount restrictions. Although, to be realistic, you should try to limit the number of players based on the amount of RAM your plan comes with so that the server retains reasonable performance and so that the server won't crash. Aim to allocate 100mb of ram per player, so if your server has 8GB of RAM, it should be able to handle 80 players

Where is my server based in?

As of right now our servers are located at Nuremberg, germany. You will be able to choose between other server locations in the future, please see our visions to learn more.

Can I get a custom domain name to use as my IP?

No, but you can purchase a domain name for $15 per year, fully setup for your server. Please contact us for more info. Alternatively, we can assist you in setting up a link between your AtlasNodes server and your domain on another registrar.

What is the internet speed?

All internet speeds are guaranteed to have around 482mbs download and 522mbs upload. This also comes with 0.23ms ping. As a bonus this measurement will get better over time as our server infrastructure grows.

What plugins can I install?

Any plugins can be installed on our servers. Generally most plugins work fine without extra set up. You can install as many plugins you want however that number can be limited by the performance of your server.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel at any time but when you do the server will stay open until the month ends. We do not refund any payments made. You can also downgrade/upgrade plans as you wish, but when downgrading you will have to pay for a new month when you downgrade.
